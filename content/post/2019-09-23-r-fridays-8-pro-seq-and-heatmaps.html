Looking for patterns in PRO-seq with heatmaps

Traditional RNA-seq measures RNA levels which are the result of both transcription and degradation of mRNA transcripts. Monica Bomber of the Hiebert Lab uses PRO-seq (precision nuclear run-on sequencing) to directly measure gene transcription rates. PRO-seq can measure transcription rates and identify transcription start sites at high-resolution througout the genome. Today, Monica showed us how to generate a heatmap to understand how transcription rates change over time in human lymphocytes.

Monica read in her data using read_csv() from the tidyverse.
